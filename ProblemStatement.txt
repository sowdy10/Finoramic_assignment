Problem: Given two strings S and T, 
find out whether any anagram of T is a Substring of S. 
DefinitionAnagram: Any permutation of string - eg. leg,gel,egl,elg are all anagrams
Substring: Continuous sequence of characters of a string
Goo is substring of Google but Gog is not. 
Example:A = Google, B = Gog Answer: FalseA= Google, B = Goo Answer: True 
A=Google, B = Got Answer: False
